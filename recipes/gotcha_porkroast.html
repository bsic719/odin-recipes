Gotcha Pork!

Description

A creamy, seasoned mashed potato dish wrapped in an abundance of oily & fatty bacon finished with a glazed red wine sace!!!

Creative invention made by the main character Soma Yukihira. This is an interpretation and a specialized rendition of this epic masterpiece.

Follow the anime at: crunchyroll
