Powerpuff Girls

Description

All things nice, and all things cute; powerpuff girls are here through and through

~stolen from Professor X~

Ingredients

  • Sugar:
    • 1/4 cup Confectionary Sugar
    • 1/4 cup Brown sugars
    • 6 sugar cubes
  • Spice:
    • 2 tsp Cinnamon
    • 1 tbsp Anise
    • 1 tbsp Apple pie spice
    • 1 tbsp Pumpkin pie spice
    • 1/4 tsp Ginger
    • 1 Jalapeno (crushed NOT SLICED)
  • Everything Nice:
    • 2 tbsp Essence of kitten (no more than 6 months)
    • 1 Sleeve girl scout Thin Mints
    • 1 tbsp Bottle rainbow (Do Not Ingest)
    • 1 tbsp Bottle love (Do Not Inhale)
  • Chemical X:
    • 1 tsp Chemical X

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix ingredients (minus the chemical X)
  2. Stir contents clockwise for 1 full minute (Not a second more or less)
  3. Finally, add in the Chemical X

*POOF* now you have powerpuff girls!
